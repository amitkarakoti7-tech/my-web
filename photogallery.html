MY DREAMS

Tungnath, located in Uttarakhand's Rudraprayag district at 3,680 meters (12,073 ft), is the world’s highest Shiva temple and the highest of the five Panch Kedar temples. Believed to be over 1,000 years old and linked to the Pandavas, it is accessed via a 3.5–4 km moderate trek from Chopta, offering panoramic Himalayan views. The temple is typically open from May to November.
