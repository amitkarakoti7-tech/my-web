Furniture Website

FurniHome is a modern and responsive furniture website designed with a clean and minimal user interface. The website provides an easy and user-friendly shopping experience for customers looking for stylish home furniture. It includes a Home page to showcase featured products, a Shop page to browse furniture collections, an About page to introduce the brand, and a Contact page for customer inquiries. The design focuses on simplicity, proper spacing, modern typography, and an attractive color palette, making it easy to navigate and visually appealing.
